⚙️ Settings & Google Sheets Sync
Connection Status
⬤ Not Connected
Step 1 — Google OAuth Client ID
From Google Cloud Console → Credentials → OAuth 2.0 Client IDs. Create one with application type "Web application".
Step 2 — Google Sheet ID
From your Sheet's URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/edit
Step 3 — Connect
After clicking Connect, Google will ask you to sign in and grant permission to read/write your spreadsheet. This only happens once.
Manual Sync
Auto-sync happens whenever you add, edit, or complete a task.
To add tasks manually in Google Sheets: add them to the FF_Tasks tab (not the styled tabs) using the same column format, then click ⬇ Pull.
